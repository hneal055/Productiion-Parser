COMMERCIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Commercial Lease Agreement (the "Lease") is made as of November 7, 2025, between Paramount Properties Group LLC ("Landlord") and Sarah's Boutique LLC ("Tenant").

PROPERTY: 1250 Main Street, Suite 200, Anytown, ST 12345
PREMISES: Approximately 1,800 square feet of retail space

1. LEASE TERM
The initial term of this Lease shall be three (3) years, commencing on December 1, 2025, and ending on November 30, 2028. This Lease shall automatically renew for successive three-year terms unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least one hundred eighty (180) days prior to the end of the then-current term.

2. RENT
(a) Base Rent: $3,500 per month for the first year
(b) Rent Escalation: Base rent shall increase by 8% annually on each anniversary date
(c) Additional Rent: Tenant shall pay additional rent as specified in Section 5
(d) Late Fee: $150 if rent not received by 5th of month, plus 2% daily interest thereafter
(e) Security Deposit: $10,500 (three months' rent), non-refundable

3. USE OF PREMISES
Tenant may use the Premises only for retail operations as approved by Landlord. Any change of use requires Landlord's written consent and payment of $2,500 administrative fee.

4. OPERATING HOURS
Tenant agrees to maintain operating hours of at least 10 AM to 8 PM, seven days per week, including all holidays except Christmas Day. Failure to maintain these hours for more than two days per month shall constitute a material breach.

5. ADDITIONAL CHARGES
Tenant shall pay the following additional charges:
(a) Common Area Maintenance (CAM): Tenant's pro-rata share, estimated at $850/month
(b) Property Taxes: Tenant's pro-rata share of all property taxes, estimated at $400/month
(c) Insurance: Tenant's pro-rata share of building insurance, estimated at $200/month
(d) HVAC Maintenance: $350/month for maintenance and repairs of all heating and cooling systems
(e) Roof and Structural Repairs: Tenant's pro-rata share of all roof repairs, replacements, and structural maintenance
(f) Parking Lot Maintenance: $150/month for snow removal, resurfacing, and line painting
(g) Utilities: All utilities including water, electric, gas, trash, and sewer
(h) Administrative Fee: $200/month for lease administration
(i) Building Improvements: Tenant's pro-rata share of any capital improvements Landlord chooses to make

Note: All estimates are subject to change at any time. Tenant will be billed for actual costs incurred.

6. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS
(a) Tenant shall be responsible for all repairs and maintenance to the Premises, including but not limited to: plumbing, electrical, HVAC, flooring, walls, ceiling, doors, windows, and all fixtures.
(b) Tenant shall maintain the exterior of the building, including painting, repairs, and landscaping adjacent to Tenant's suite.
(c) Tenant shall be responsible for all pest control.
(d) Landlord reserves the right to make repairs and charge costs to Tenant if Landlord determines repairs are necessary.

7. INSURANCE AND LIABILITY
(a) Tenant shall maintain general liability insurance with minimum coverage of $5,000,000 per occurrence and name Landlord as additional insured.
(b) Tenant shall be liable for any and all damages, injuries, or losses occurring on or related to the Premises, regardless of cause.
(c) Tenant releases Landlord from all liability for any damage or injury to persons or property, including damage caused by fire, water, weather, or Landlord's negligence.
(d) Tenant shall indemnify and hold Landlord harmless from any claims, including those arising from Landlord's own acts or omissions.

8. ALTERATIONS
Tenant may not make any alterations, improvements, or installations without Landlord's prior written consent. All alterations, once made, become property of Landlord. Tenant must pay for removal of any alterations at end of lease term if Landlord requests, and restore Premises to original condition.

9. ASSIGNMENT AND SUBLETTING
Tenant may not assign this Lease or sublet the Premises without Landlord's written consent. If consent is granted, Tenant must pay Landlord 50% of any profits from sublease and pay $3,000 administrative fee.

10. TERMINATION BY LANDLORD
Landlord may terminate this Lease with sixty (60) days written notice if:
(a) Landlord wishes to demolish or substantially renovate the building
(b) Landlord receives an offer from another tenant for higher rent
(c) Landlord wishes to occupy the space for Landlord's own business
(d) For any other reason deemed appropriate by Landlord

11. TERMINATION BY TENANT
Tenant may terminate this Lease only by:
(a) Providing twelve (12) months written notice
(b) Paying early termination fee equal to all remaining rent due under the Lease term
(c) Paying $15,000 administrative fee
(d) Restoring Premises to original condition at Tenant's expense

12. DEFAULT AND REMEDIES
If Tenant defaults under any provision of this Lease, Landlord may:
(a) Immediately terminate the Lease
(b) Accelerate all remaining rent due under the full Lease term
(c) Enter and re-let the Premises and charge Tenant for any shortfall
(d) Hold Tenant liable for all costs, including attorney fees, court costs, and lost profits
(e) Retain security deposit

13. HOLDOVER
If Tenant remains in Premises after Lease termination, Tenant shall pay 200% of the then-current monthly rent for each month of holdover.

14. RULES AND REGULATIONS
Landlord may establish rules and regulations for the building from time to time. Violation of any rule shall constitute a material breach of this Lease.

15. SUBORDINATION
This Lease is subordinate to any and all mortgages, deeds of trust, or other liens now or hereafter placed on the property.

16. PERSONAL GUARANTEE
Tenant's principal owners (Sarah Martinez and David Chen) personally and unconditionally guarantee all obligations under this Lease and shall remain liable even after Lease termination.

17. GOVERNING LAW
This Lease shall be governed by the laws of the State where the property is located. Tenant consents to exclusive jurisdiction in Landlord's chosen venue.

RED FLAGS IN THIS CONTRACT:
- 8% annual rent increase (very high)
- 180-day notice required to avoid auto-renewal
- Non-refundable security deposit (should be refundable!)
- Required operating hours 7 days/week including holidays
- Hidden costs: HVAC, roof, structural repairs on tenant
- Unlimited liability for tenant
- CAM charges with no cap ("actual costs")
- One-sided termination (Landlord: 60 days, Tenant: 12 months + all remaining rent!)
- Early termination fee equals ALL remaining rent
- 200% holdover rent
- Tenant liable even for landlord's negligence
- Personal guarantee extends beyond lease term
- Landlord can terminate to get higher-paying tenant
- All improvements become landlord's property
- Tenant pays for removal of improvements
- 50% of sublease profits go to landlord
