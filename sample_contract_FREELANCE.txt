FREELANCE SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Freelance Service Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of November 7, 2025, between Digital Innovations LLC ("Client") and Jane Designer ("Contractor").

1. SERVICES
Contractor agrees to provide graphic design services including logo design, website mockups, and marketing materials as requested by Client from time to time.

2. TERM
This Agreement shall commence on November 7, 2025, and continue for an initial term of twelve (12) months. The Agreement will automatically renew for successive twelve-month periods unless Client provides written notice of termination at least one hundred twenty (120) days prior to the end of the then-current term.

3. COMPENSATION
Client agrees to pay Contractor as follows:
(a) Base rate: $50 per hour for design work
(b) Payment terms: Net 90 (payment due ninety days after invoice date)
(c) Rush fees: Additional 25% for work requested with less than 72 hours notice
(d) Revisions: First two rounds of revisions included; additional revisions billed at $75/hour
(e) Client reserves the right to adjust rates with thirty (30) days notice

4. EXPENSES
Contractor shall be responsible for all expenses incurred in performing services, including but not limited to software licenses, stock images, fonts, and any other materials or tools required.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
(a) All work product, designs, concepts, sketches, preliminary designs, final designs, and any other materials created by Contractor, whether or not used by Client, shall be the exclusive property of Client.
(b) This includes any work product created prior to the execution of this Agreement that is similar to or could be used in connection with Client's business.
(c) Contractor agrees to assign all rights, title, and interest in such work product to Client, including all copyrights, trademarks, and other intellectual property rights.
(d) Contractor waives all moral rights in the work product.
(e) Contractor shall not use any work product created under this Agreement for Contractor's portfolio without prior written consent from Client.

6. NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION
(a) During the term of this Agreement and for thirty-six (36) months thereafter, Contractor shall not provide design services to any company in the technology, marketing, or e-commerce industries.
(b) Contractor shall not solicit or accept work from any of Client's customers, partners, or vendors during the term and for twenty-four (24) months after termination.

7. CONFIDENTIALITY
Contractor agrees to maintain confidentiality of all Client information, including but not limited to business strategies, customer information, financial data, and any other information disclosed during the engagement.

8. TERMINATION
(a) Client may terminate this Agreement at any time with thirty (30) days written notice for any reason or no reason.
(b) Contractor may terminate this Agreement only with one hundred eighty (180) days written notice and payment of early termination fee equal to three months of average billings.
(c) Upon termination, Contractor must immediately deliver all work product, whether complete or incomplete, and all Client materials.

9. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS
Contractor is an independent contractor and not an employee of Client. However, Contractor agrees to work exclusively for Client during normal business hours (9 AM - 6 PM EST, Monday-Friday) and be available for meetings and calls as requested.

10. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION
(a) Contractor shall be liable for any and all damages, losses, or expenses incurred by Client arising from Contractor's work, including but not limited to copyright infringement, trademark violations, or defects in the work product.
(b) Contractor agrees to indemnify and hold harmless Client from any claims, damages, or expenses, including reasonable attorney fees.
(c) Contractor's liability under this Agreement is unlimited.

11. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Any disputes shall be resolved by binding arbitration in Client's home jurisdiction. Contractor agrees to pay all arbitration costs and fees. The prevailing party shall be entitled to recover attorney fees and costs.

12. MODIFICATIONS
Client may modify this Agreement at any time by providing written notice to Contractor. Continued performance by Contractor after notice of modification constitutes acceptance of the modified terms.

13. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This Agreement represents the entire understanding between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements.

RED FLAGS IN THIS CONTRACT:
- Net 90 payment terms (3-month wait for payment!)
- Automatic renewal with 120-day notice required
- One-sided termination (client: 30 days, contractor: 180 days + fee)
- Ownership of ALL work product, even pre-existing and unused work
- Cannot use work in portfolio without permission
- 36-month non-compete (extremely broad and long)
- 24-month non-solicitation
- Unlimited liability for contractor
- Client can unilaterally change rates and terms
- "Exclusive" work during business hours (not really independent contractor)
- Contractor pays arbitration costs
- IP assignment includes work created BEFORE the agreement
