🎬 FILMING TIPS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

BEFORE RECORDING:
□ Close unnecessary browser tabs
□ Hide bookmarks bar
□ Use incognito/private mode (clean browser)
□ Set browser to 1280x720 or 1920x1080
□ Have demo file ready to upload
□ Clear your downloads folder (for Excel demo)
□ Test audio levels
□ Have glass of water nearby

DURING RECORDING:
□ Speak slowly and clearly
□ Pause between sections (easier to edit)
□ Smile (it shows in your voice!)
□ Move mouse smoothly and deliberately
□ Let charts/pages load fully before speaking
□ HOVER on charts to show interactivity (this is key!)
□ Don't rush - 5 minutes is perfect

AFTER RECORDING:
□ Watch full video
□ Check audio quality
□ Verify all features shown clearly
□ Add captions if possible (helps SEO)
□ Export in 1080p if possible

IF YOU MAKE A MISTAKE:
- Just pause, then start that sentence again
- You can edit it out later
- Or just keep going - authentic is better than perfect!
