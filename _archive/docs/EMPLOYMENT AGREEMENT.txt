EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of November 7, 2025, between InnovateTech Solutions Inc. ("Company") and Michael Rodriguez ("Employee").

1. POSITION AND DUTIES
Employee is hired as Senior Software Engineer. Employee shall perform such duties as assigned by Company from time to time and shall devote full time and attention to Company's business.

2. TERM
This is an at-will employment relationship. Either party may terminate employment at any time for any reason or no reason.

3. COMPENSATION
(a) Base Salary: $95,000 per year, payable bi-weekly
(b) Annual Review: Salary may be reviewed annually at Company's discretion
(c) Bonus: Employee may be eligible for discretionary annual bonus of up to 15% of base salary, subject to Company performance and Employee performance
(d) All compensation is subject to applicable taxes and withholdings

4. BENEFITS
Employee shall be eligible for Company benefits including health insurance, 401(k) plan, and paid time off, subject to the terms of Company's benefit plans as may be amended from time to time.

5. PAID TIME OFF
Employee shall accrue ten (10) days of paid time off per year. Unused PTO does not carry over and is forfeited at year end. Upon termination of employment, no payout will be made for unused PTO.

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
(a) Employee agrees that all inventions, discoveries, ideas, improvements, software, code, designs, and works of authorship (collectively "Work Product") conceived, developed, or created by Employee, whether during working hours or not, and whether using Company resources or not, shall be the sole and exclusive property of Company.
(b) This includes Work Product that relates to Company's current or reasonably anticipated business, research, or development.
(c) Employee agrees to assign and does hereby assign all right, title, and interest in such Work Product to Company.
(d) Employee shall assist Company in obtaining patents, copyrights, or other protection for such Work Product at Company's request, at no additional compensation.
(e) Employee agrees that all Work Product shall be considered "works made for hire" under copyright law.

7. PRIOR INVENTIONS
Employee represents that Schedule A attached hereto lists all inventions, discoveries, and works created by Employee prior to employment with Company. Any items not listed shall be deemed assigned to Company.

8. CONFIDENTIALITY
Employee acknowledges that during employment, Employee will have access to confidential and proprietary information including trade secrets, customer lists, business strategies, financial information, and technical data. Employee agrees to maintain strict confidentiality of all such information during and after employment.

9. NON-COMPETITION
(a) During employment and for eighteen (18) months following termination of employment for any reason, Employee shall not, directly or indirectly:
    (i) Work for, consult with, or provide services to any company engaged in software development, cloud computing, artificial intelligence, data analytics, or any business competitive with Company
    (ii) Solicit or service any customers or clients of Company
    (iii) Solicit or hire any employees or contractors of Company
(b) This restriction applies within a 100-mile radius of any Company office or within any geographic area where Company does business.
(c) Employee acknowledges this restriction is reasonable and necessary to protect Company's legitimate business interests.

10. SOCIAL MEDIA AND PUBLIC STATEMENTS
Employee shall not post, publish, or make any statements about Company, its products, employees, or customers on social media or any public forum without prior written approval from Company. This applies during and after employment.

11. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
(a) Employee agrees that any disputes arising from or related to employment shall be resolved through binding arbitration.
(b) Arbitration shall be conducted in Company's home jurisdiction under rules selected by Company.
(c) Employee waives any right to trial by jury.
(d) Employee waives any right to bring or participate in class action lawsuits.
(e) The arbitrator may not award punitive damages.
(f) Each party shall bear their own costs, but if Company prevails, Employee shall reimburse Company's attorney fees.

12. OWNERSHIP OF WORK
Employee acknowledges that any work performed using Company equipment, software, or resources, or during Company time, is the property of Company. This includes emails, documents, code, and any other materials.

13. REMOTE WORK
While Company currently permits remote work, this is a privilege that may be revoked at any time. Employee may be required to report to office with 48 hours notice.

14. OVERTIME
Employee is classified as exempt and not eligible for overtime pay. Employee acknowledges that the position may require work beyond standard 40-hour workweek, including evenings and weekends, with no additional compensation.

15. BACKGROUND CHECK AND MONITORING
Employee consents to background checks and ongoing monitoring of Company systems, including email, internet usage, and computer activity. Employee has no expectation of privacy when using Company resources.

16. MODIFICATION OF TERMS
Company reserves the right to modify the terms of this Agreement, including compensation and benefits, at any time with written notice. Continued employment constitutes acceptance of modifications.

17. SEVERABILITY
If any provision of this Agreement is found to be unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

18. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This Agreement contains the entire understanding between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements, whether written or oral, including any statements made during the interview process.

19. ACKNOWLEDGMENTS
Employee acknowledges that:
(a) Employee has read and understands this Agreement
(b) Employee has had opportunity to consult with an attorney
(c) Employee enters into this Agreement voluntarily
(d) Employee understands employment is at-will despite this Agreement

RED FLAGS IN THIS CONTRACT:
- Ownership of ALL inventions, even those created at home on personal time
- Broad non-compete (18 months, 100-mile radius, entire industry)
- No PTO payout on termination or carryover
- No PTO accrual mentioned for first year
- Company can unilaterally change terms (compensation, benefits)
- One-sided arbitration (Company picks rules and location)
- Class action waiver
- Forced reimbursement of attorney fees if Company wins
- Prior inventions must be listed or deemed assigned
- Very broad confidentiality (covers "business strategies")
- Social media restrictions extend after employment
- Ownership of personal emails if using company equipment
- At-will employment but restrictive non-compete
- 48-hour notice for office return
- Exempt status but may require extensive overtime
- No severance terms
- IP assignment covers "reasonably anticipated" business (very broad)
- Non-solicitation of employees for 18 months
